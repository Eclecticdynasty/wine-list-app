Malbec

Taste Profile:

  • Dry
  • Full Body
  • Medium Tannin
  • 13.5 - 15% ABV

Food Pairings:

Malbec is a great choice with leaner red meats and does wonders with melted blue cheese.

French Malbec

In France, Malbec tends to have more structure, firmer tannins, and an inky dark, brooding quality.

Agentinian Malbec

A Malbec from Argentina tends to be plummy and fruit-forward, with a velvety soft texture.
