Riesling

Taste Profile:

  • Off-Dry
  • Light Body
  • Low/None Tannin
  • Under 10% ABV

Food Pairings:

Off-dry Riesling wines make a great pairing to spicy Indian and Asian cuisines and do excellently alongside duck, pork, bacon, shrimp and crab.

How to Find a Dry Riesling

When looking for a drier style Riesling, look for one with moderate levels of alcohol (e.g. 11% ABV and above). An ABV of 9%, for example, usually indicates that not all the sugars were converting into alcohol, leaving some leftover in the form of residual sugar.
