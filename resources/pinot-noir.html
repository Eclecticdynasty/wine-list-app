French Pinot Noir

Pinot Noir wines are made in a few places throughout France but the most famous region is Burgundy. When tasting French Pinot Noir, you may note its more earthy and floral style. This is part of Burgundy’s terroir.

California Pinot Noir

While California might otherwise be too warm for this grape, you’ll find Pinot Noir excels in places that receive cooling breezes (and morning fog) from the Pacific Ocean. The ocean moderates the temperatures in places like Sonoma, Southern Napa Valley, and the Central Coast (including Santa Barbara). California Pinot Noir is typically rich, fruity, and lush in style.
